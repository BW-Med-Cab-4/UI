Features

MedCab is your personal, virtual, alternative medicine cabinet. It is a powerful app that lets you search for strains of medical marijana, finds the right dose, and saves your products. MedCab will help you discover the right strains and doses for your needs and keeps that information at your fingertips. Enter your infomation, get product recommendations, and save your favorites.
